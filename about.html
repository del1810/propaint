The Exhibition

About PROPAINT

PROPAINT is a dedicated trade exhibition exclusively focused on Finished Paint Products, Decorative Paint Products, Premium Consumer Paint Solutions, Designer Wall Finishes, Surface Aesthetic Technologies, Waterproofing & Protective Paint Systems, Wood Coatings, Industrial Finished Coatings, Automotive Finishing, Marine Protection Coatings and Powder Coatings.

Positioned as South India's largest business platform for the finished paint products industry, PROPAINT connects leading brands, manufacturers, distributors, dealers, architects, contractors, interior designers, builders, industrial buyers and consumers under one dynamic marketplace.

With South India continuing to expand in infrastructure, urban development, manufacturing and industrial investment, PROPAINT creates a focused platform where the region's paint and coatings industry can meet, source, collaborate and grow.
