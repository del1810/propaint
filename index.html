About the Expo

About PROPAINT

PROPAINT is a dedicated trade exhibition exclusively focused on Finished Paint Products, Decorative Paint Products, Premium Consumer Paint Solutions, Designer Wall Finishes, Surface Aesthetic Technologies and Waterproofing & Protective Paint Systems.

Positioned as South India's largest business platform for the finished paint products industry, PROPAINT connects leading brands, manufacturers, distributors, dealers, architects, contractors, interior designers, builders, industrial buyers and consumers under one dynamic marketplace.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Vision: To establish PROPAINT as India's leading and most influential paints and coating products exhibition — the region's premier destination for business growth, technology advancement and future-ready industry partnerships.

Mission: To create India's most focused and future-ready business platform for the paint products, coatings and surface finishing industry — connecting manufacturers, suppliers, distributors and institutional buyers to accelerate trade, innovation and industrial growth.
